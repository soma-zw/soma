For Headmasters & Teachers

Soma Portal

The command centre for your school

Soma Portal is a powerful Electron desktop application and web dashboard that gives headmasters, deputy heads, class teachers, and bursars everything they need to run the school — in one window.

  • Live student records: grades, attendance, health, co-curriculars
  • Exam results entry with auto-calculated grade boundaries (ZIMSEC, Cambridge)
  • Fee management: statements, payments, outstanding balances
  • Staff management: HR, timetables, leave tracking
  • Automated parent notifications
  • Role-based access: headmaster, teacher, bursar views

For Parents

Soma Connect

Stay connected to your child's school life

Soma Connect is a mobile app for parents that puts everything they need to know about their child — in their pocket. Real-time updates, direct messaging, fee information, and academic results without a single phone call to the school office.

  • View your child's current results and progress
  • Check fee statements and outstanding balances
  • Receive push notifications from the school
  • Direct messaging with class teachers
  • Link multiple children across different schools
  • Available on Android & iOS

For Students

Soma X

Your school, your way

Soma X is the student-facing experience: a smart browser, study tools, AI assistance, and access to your academic profile — all in one app designed for how Zimbabwean students actually learn and work.

  • AI-powered study assistant
  • Smart browser with tab management and incognito mode
  • View your results and class position
  • Bookmarks, history, and a personalised dashboard
  • Creative projects and competitions (Soma Creative Lab)
  • Built for Zimbabwe's ZIMSEC curriculum

Across All Products

Soma AI

Intelligence woven into every product

Soma AI isn't a separate product — it's the intelligence layer running across the entire platform. Ask questions in plain English, get automated reports, receive smart suggestions, and let the system surface insights you'd never find manually.

  • Natural language queries: "How many students failed Maths this term?"
  • Automatic grade comment generation for report cards
  • Fee reminder automation
  • AI study assistant for Soma X students
  • Predictive attendance and performance flags

One shared backend. Always in sync.

All Soma products connect to a single Supabase backend. A fee payment recorded in Portal appears instantly in a parent's Connect app. A grade entered by a teacher is visible to the student in Soma X — in real time.
