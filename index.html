What is Soma

One platform.
Every part of your school.

Soma is an AI-powered school management system built for Zimbabwean institutions. It connects administration, finance, teachers, and students into a single, intelligent workspace.

Ask questions in plain language. Get real answers. No more digging through registers or calling the bursar's office for a fee balance.
