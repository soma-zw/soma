Download and install Soma Connect for your operating system. Get instant access to the digital backbone of your school.

Installation & Setup

Download the installer
Choose the version for your operating system above and click the download button.
Run the installer
Open the downloaded file and follow the installation wizard. The app will automatically update to the latest version.
Sign in with your school account
Launch Soma Connect and sign in using your school credentials to access your dashboard.

Soma Connect automatically checks for updates and installs them in the background. No manual updates needed.
