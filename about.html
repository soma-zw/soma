Our Story

Why we built Soma

We're two young Zimbabwean co-founders who grew up watching school administration drown in paperwork. Registers, physical fee books, written notices home — the work was enormous and errors were inevitable.

We built Soma because we believed Zimbabwean schools deserve the same quality of digital infrastructure as any school in London or Singapore. Not a foreign platform poorly adapted, but something designed from scratch for our context — our curriculum, our fee structures, our parent-teacher relationships.

We build publicly. We ship fast. We listen to headmasters and bursars and parents and students. Soma is the product of that listening.

What We Do

One platform. Every part of the school.

Soma is a suite of AI-powered tools: Soma Portal for headmasters and teachers, Soma Connect for parents, and Soma X for students. All powered by the same intelligent backend, all sharing the same data in real time.

From entering exam results to tracking fee payments to messaging parents — every workflow your school already has, now digital, fast, and connected.

Our Values

What we stand for

Local First

We build for Zimbabwe's specific curriculum, ZIMSEC grading, and institutional culture. Not a generic product — a Zimbabwean one.

Ship Fast, Learn Fast

We build in public, release early, and iterate with real feedback from the schools using Soma daily.

Trust & Security

Student data is sacred. Every part of Soma is built with privacy-first architecture and strict access controls.

Student-Centred

Every feature we build is ultimately for the student. Better data means better decisions means better outcomes.

Community Driven

Schools, teachers, parents — they're co-builders of Soma. Our suggestion hub exists for a reason.

Africa-Wide Vision

We start in Zimbabwe, but Africa's 600 million students deserve this. We're building the foundation for a continent-scale platform.
