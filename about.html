What is Soma?

If you search for the word "Soma," you will find that it translates beautifully to "study" or "learn" in both the Shona and Swahili languages. As a platform, Soma represents an AI-powered foundation for the future of African education.

Explore the platform

The Origin

Meaning "to study" or "to read" in Shona, the name reflects our core mission: digitizing administrative workflows so schools and students can focus entirely on learning, rather than paperwork.

One ID Architecture

Before Soma, a student transferring schools meant lost academic history. We created the "One ID" system to ensure a learner's complete story travels safely with them from Grade 1 to Form 4.

Offline Capabilities

We understand that connectivity can drop. Our system is built to run reliably on local school networks, allowing staff to enter results offline and automatically sync securely once restored.

Built by visionaries.
Driven by necessity.

Soma was founded by Tanatswa Chiyangwa and Arthur Tachiona. At just 19 years old, these two young innovators looked at the fragmented educational infrastructure in Zimbabwe and decided to code a better future. What began as a passionate project to track student data efficiently has rapidly evolved into an AI-powered ecosystem trusted by top institutions. They understand the system perfectly, because they just graduated from it.
