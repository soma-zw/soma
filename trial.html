001 — The problem

Every transfer starts
the record from zero.

Reports go missing in the move between schools. Fee balances get re-entered by hand, term after term. A learner who has been at school for six years arrives at a new gate as a stranger, and the office starts a new file.

002 — The idea

One ID. Every school
that runs Soma.

Each learner gets a Soma ID at enrolment. Results, fee history, attendance and notes travel with it for as long as they're in school — even when the school changes.

Transfers only move with the sending school's approval.
